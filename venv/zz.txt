<P class=MsoNormal><B style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal"><U>Foreign Registration Expired—Proof of Renewal Required</U></B><o:p></o:p></P>
<P class=MsoNormal><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><A name=fpa_K10_1_10182019_81753AM><SPAN style='mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"'>The application specifies Trademark Act Section 44(e) as the sole filing basis.<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN><I style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal">See</I> 15 U.S.C. §1</SPAN>126(e); 37 C.F.R. §2.34(a)(3). </A><SPAN style="mso-bookmark: fpa_K10_1_10182019_81753AM"><SPAN style='mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"'>Although the application includes a copy of a foreign registration, the foreign registration on which this application is based </SPAN>expired</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bookmark: fpa_K10_1_10182019_81753AM"><SPAN style='mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"'> on </SPAN>January 13, 2003</SPAN><SPAN style="mso-bookmark: fpa_K10_1_10182019_81753AM"><SPAN style='mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"'>.<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN>For an application filed under Section 44(e), the foreign registration must be in force at the time the United States issues a registration based on that foreign registration.<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN>37 C.F.R. §2.34(a)(3)(iii); <I style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal">In re Societe D’Exploitation de la Marque Le Fouquet’s</I>, 67 USPQ2d 1784, 1788-89 (TTAB 2003); TMEP §1004.01(a).<o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="mso-bookmark: fpa_K10_1_10182019_81753AM"><SPAN style='mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"'><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P>
<P class=MsoNormal style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify"><SPAN style="mso-bookmark: fpa_K10_1_10182019_81753AM"><SPAN style='mso-bidi-font-family: "Times New Roman"'>Therefore, applicant must provide a certificate of renewal or other certification from the intellectual property office of the foreign country or a copy of the foreign registration that shows that the foreign registration has been renewed and will be in force at the time the registration issues in the United States.<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN>37 C.F.R. §2.34(a)(3)(iii); TMEP §1004.01(a).<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN>If the certificate of renewal or other certification is not written in English, applicant must also provide an English translation.<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN><I style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal">See</I> 37 C.F.R. §2.34(a)(3)(iii); TMEP §1004.01(a)-(b).<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN>The translation should be signed by the translator.<SPAN style="mso-spacerun: yes">&nbsp; </SPAN><I style="mso-bidi-font-style: normal">See</I> TMEP §1004.01(b).<o:p></o:p></SPAN></SPAN></P><SPAN style="mso-bookmark: fpa_K10_1_10182019_81753AM"></SPAN>
